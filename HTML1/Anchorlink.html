Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC) is the parent college of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions, Hyderabad, Telangana, India founded by Ch Malla Reddy. The institute was established in 2002, as approved by the AICTE New Delhi, and was affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH). In 2008, the college was accredited by NBA. It has also been certified by NAAC as an A-grade institution in the Hyderabad region.[1] The college was granted permanent affiliation and autonomous status by JNTU in 2011.
